Care

  • Hand-wash only; wipe dry straight after use.
  • Stand upright to allow airflow.
  • Refresh occasionally with food-safe board oil or wax.
  • Avoid soaking, dishwasher, or harsh detergents.

About the maker

Noah is a 16-year-old maker on the Gold Coast who genuinely loves working with timber. What started as a small project in his family workshop has grown into a passion for creating solid, practical pieces that are made to last.

Every board is carefully glued, shaped, sanded and finished by hand by Noah himself — no factory shortcuts, no mass production. Each one reflects the time, care and pride he puts into his work, which is why no two boards are ever exactly the same.
