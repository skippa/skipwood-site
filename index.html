Details & materials

Solid hardwood, finished by hand

Noah selects reclaimed Australian hardwoods, matches grain and colour, then laminates, shapes and finishes each board by hand. The result is a heavy, stable chopping board that feels good under the knife and looks even better on the bench.

  • Approx 500 × 300 × 40 mm, around 4.5 kg
  • Reclaimed Australian hardwoods, food-safe oil and beeswax finish
  • Chamfered edges for comfort and easier lifting
  • Non-slip rubber feet to keep the board steady on your bench
  • Each board is unique – no two grain patterns are the same
